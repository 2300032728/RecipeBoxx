Paneer Tikka Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 250g Paneer (cottage cheese), cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup thick yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp mustard oil
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp garam masala powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 1-2 tbsp oil or butter for grilling

Procedure:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, add the yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, mustard oil, and all the dry spices (turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, cumin powder). Add salt to taste and mix everything well.
  2. Cut the paneer into cubes and add them to the marinade. Coat the paneer pieces well with the marinade. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to 1 hour for the flavors to absorb.
  3. Preheat your grill or oven to 200°C (390°F). If using a grill, skewer the marinated paneer cubes onto metal or wooden skewers. If using an oven, place the marinated paneer cubes on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
  4. Brush the paneer cubes with a little oil or butter to keep them moist while grilling.
  5. Grill the paneer tikka for 15-20 minutes, or until the paneer is slightly charred and golden on the edges. You can also baste the pieces with a little more butter or oil halfway through grilling.
  6. Once done, remove from the grill/oven and garnish with fresh coriander leaves and a squeeze of lemon juice.
  7. Serve hot with mint chutney or a tangy tamarind dip.
